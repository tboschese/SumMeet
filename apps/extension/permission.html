Enable your microphone for SumMeet

SumMeet needs your microphone so your own voice is recorded alongside the meeting audio. Grant it once here — Chrome remembers it for the extension.

If it keeps failing: make sure Chrome itself is allowed to use the mic in macOS System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone, then try again. On Windows, check Settings → Privacy → Microphone. You can close this tab once you see “enabled”.
